About me

I started a PhD in Philosophy at New York University in the fall of 2011. I specialize in philosophy of language, but I'm very interested in the points where it intersects with the philosophy of mind. I'm also a big fan of Thomas Reid.

I'm currently writing my dissertation. There, I examine some aspects of a phenomenon I call semantic variation and develop a novel account of speech and attitude ascriptions. I also put some pressure on standard accounts of agreement, communication, and the content of `that'-clauses. If you're intrigued, email me at: martin.abreu@nyu.edu.
